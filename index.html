Swing!

What is this?

It's a pendulum controlled by a neural network.

That network is a component of an algorithm called DDPG (Deep Deterministic Policy Gradients). Sounds fancy, doesn't it?

Why does it keep reappearing?

The pendulum is simulated in episodes.

Each episode is 200 frames long. When an episode completes, the pendulum resets at a random position.

Restarting at a random location gives the network a chance to learn and allows us to evaluate the performance of the algorithm.
